Welcome to Love Desserts

Sweets that will melt your heart

A Dessert Paradise

In many countries, the sweet flavor is considered to harmonize emotions. Love Desserts is therefore the place to go for true comfort good. By comfort food, I mean a place where you can eat good quality pastries and ice cream for no other reason other than to just drown your stresses in unabashed culinary goodness.


Buffets have now become a vital part of a modern Filipino. A lot of food spots which offer buffets are exclusive of cuisines for main courses. Take Love Desserts for example - a dessert buffet paradise! Selections include an assortment of cakes, cupcakes, brownies, variations of Brazo de Mercedes, creme brûlée and even cookies and ice cream. There is the universally Filipino halo halo selection bar and a generous variety of ice cream flavors to choose from.
